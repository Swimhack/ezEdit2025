Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +
You get 7 days of full access to browse and edit files. No credit card required. After the trial, you'll need to upgrade to save changes to your server.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What FTP servers are supported? +
EzEdit works with any standard FTP server including shared hosting, VPS, dedicated servers, and cloud platforms like AWS, DigitalOcean, and more.
Is the lifetime plan really lifetime? +
Yes! The lifetime plan gives you permanent access to EzEdit for one domain. You'll receive all future updates at no additional cost.
How does AI assistance work? +
Klein, our AI assistant, analyzes your code and provides contextual help. It can explain functions, suggest improvements, fix bugs, and help write new features using the latest AI technology.
